Quick update: **Cite-Agent v1.5.7 just dropped** with formatting fixes that make everything look way better.

## The Fix in 10 Seconds

**Before:**
```
Mean: 120.0000, Revenue: 1234567890, P/E: $\boxed{15.5000}$
```

**After:**
```
Mean: 120, Revenue: 1,234,567,890, P/E: 15.5
```

That's it. Numbers look like numbers. No decimal spam, no LaTeX weirdness, no random backticks.

## What Changed

Three fixes:
1. **Smart number formatting** - integers stay integers, big numbers get commas
2. **Killed LaTeX notation** - no more `$\boxed{}$` in your terminal
3. **Cleaned markdown artifacts** - stripped stray backticks

Everything just looks cleaner now.
